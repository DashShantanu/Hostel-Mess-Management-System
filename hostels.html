What we provide

All your necessities taken care of!

Multiple-beds rooms

Never again worry about how many people you're gonna stay with! You can now choose your number of roommates, with 1, 2, 3, or 4-seater rooms available with ample space for all the members to stay comfortably.

Comfortable beds and optimal study desks

Forget about losing sleep due to a wierd bed or losing marks due to an uncomfortable study desk! We bring to you our carefully designed study desks and extremely comfortable beds so that your life can be made easier.

Free Wi-Fi and AC rooms

We have a wide range of Wi-Fi and AC rooms to choose from, so that you can enjoy your stay in the hostel without any worry of losing out on online study or the rising temperatures of the summer!

Nutritous meals and flexible menus

Missing out on your delicious home cooked meals? Well, not anymore! Our mess provides you both quality and tasteful meals. We also have flexible menus so that you can choose your meals according to your needs!

Laundary service and Hostel kits

Don't worry about wasting time with washing and drying clothes anymore! We've got you covered with our laundry service that regularly takes care of your clothes. We also provide a hostel kit consisting of quality clothes, bedsheets and other essential items that you need to have in your room.

Feedback and Student Representatives

We have a team of student sepresentatives who are always available to help you with any queries and grievances you may have. We focus on taking constructive feedbacks from students to improve the quality of the hostel.

24-hour security

We provide latest computerized 24-hour security system all around the hostel to ensure that you are safe and secure from any threats being away from home. Any medical emergency is also covered by us and it shall be taken care of swiftly and efficiently.
